# Population Dynamics · POP

UD0's **first Life Science sphere** — *the real psychohistory*.
Live: https://davidwise01.github.io/population-dynamics/

Asimov dreamed of **psychohistory**: a mathematics that predicts a civilization the way kinetic theory predicts a gas — never the molecule, always the bulk. Its real, non-fictional cousin lives in the **life sciences**: we genuinely forecast living populations — predator-prey cycles, epidemics, allele frequencies — because a population of *unaware* organisms behaves like a gas. This sphere is the honest answer to *"is psychohistory real?"*

Standing template — **The Arc · IS PSYCHOHISTORY REAL? · Real or Fluff · The Message** — cited (10 sources). The real models: Malthus 1798, Verhulst's logistic 1838, Lotka-Volterra 1925/26, Kermack-McKendrick's SIR 1927 & R₀, Hardy-Weinberg 1908, May's chaos 1976. The bridge: psychohistory needs a population that's huge **and unaware** — and self-aware people read the forecast and change it (Merton's self-fulfilling prophecy, the Lucas critique, Goodhart's law), which is why human psychohistory stays fiction. **We can forecast the flu but not the Mule, because we are not gas.** Honest edges flagged: "carrying capacity"/K is anachronistic to Verhulst; R₀ is context-dependent, not a constant; the demographic transition is description, not law; even a perfect model can go chaotic.
